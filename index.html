About Vivanta Township

Vivanta Township is a premium residential development in Banswara, Rajasthan, offering luxurious living spaces designed for modern families. Our township is strategically located on Ojhariya Bypass Road, providing easy connectivity to all major city areas while maintaining a serene environment.

With a focus on sustainability, community, and luxury, Vvanta Township brings together the best of contemporary architecture and traditional values. Our development spans over 25 acres of lush greenery, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty and modern amenities.

The township features a thoughtfully planned layout with wide roads, green belts, dedicated play areas, and state-of-the-art infrastructure. Each residential unit is designed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring optimal ventilation, natural light, and privacy.

About Vivanta Developers

Vivanta Developers is a renowned real estate development company with over 25 years of experience in creating premium residential and commercial properties across Rajasthan. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry.

We specialize in creating sustainable communities that enhance the quality of life for our residents. Our team of architects, engineers, and designers work together to create spaces that are not just beautiful but also functional and environmentally responsible.

At Vivanta Township, we have applied our decades of expertise to create a residential paradise that sets new standards for luxury living in Banswara. Every aspect of the township has been carefully planned and executed to ensure the highest quality standards.

Master Plan

Vivanta Township has been meticulously planned to create a harmonious balance between built spaces and natural environment. Our master plan focuses on creating a sustainable community that promotes healthy living and social interaction.

The township is divided into distinct zones - residential, recreational, commercial, and green zones - each designed to serve specific needs while maintaining overall harmony. Wide tree-lined avenues connect different parts of the township, encouraging walking and cycling.

Development Timeline

Follow our journey from concept to completion

2018

Concept & Planning

Initial concept development, land acquisition, and master planning. Environmental impact assessment and regulatory approvals obtained.

2019

Infrastructure Development

Development of roads, drainage, water supply, electrical infrastructure, and green spaces. Foundation work for residential blocks began.

2020

Construction Phase 1

Completion of first residential block with 50 luxury villas. Amenities like clubhouse and swimming pool construction began.

2021

First Residents Move In

First phase residents moved into their new homes. Community amenities operational. Construction of second phase began.

2022

Phase 2 Completion

Completion of farmhouses and penthouses. All major amenities including shopping complex and medical center became operational.

2023

Final Phase & Township Inauguration

Completion of final residential phase. Township officially inaugurated with 90% occupancy. Community established with active resident association.
